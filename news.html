Launch of S3ESunday, June 9th, 2013

At long last, My Little Pony: Roleplaying is Magic, Season Three Edition has launched!

With a more streamlined and player-friendly system, greatly expanded content, more playable races, new pre-made adventures, and the same commitment to staying true to canon, S3E is our best edition yet!

Round up a copy of your own, and start an adventure with your friends to discover the magic of friendship!

We invite you to visit the forums, register and say hello! And for you world-builders and storytellers, try your hand at developing an Episode or campaign setting! Players and GM's, let us know how your MLP:RiM game(s) are doing! Give us feedback, or make art, or just get involved in general!

Thank you everyone for visiting our site and taking an interest in our project. We've worked hard to make S3E the best we can make it, and we hope you'll like it as much as we do. Happy roleplaying, my little ponies!

Your faithful game designers,

~The MLP:RiM Team

Launch of S2ESaturday, May 26th, 2012

Welcome, everypony, to the official launch of My Little Pony: Roleplaying is Magic, Season Two Edition! It's been a long several months of development to get here, and we'd like to take a few moments to thank all of you for your support and encouragement and offers to help. That kind of caring is what keeps us doing what we do, and we hope that S2E exceeds your expectations and really knocks your socks off!

A few notes on the site, now that it has gone live. Use the links above to get to the S2E page, and you'll be able to get the S2E rulebook in a variety of formats—from hi-res .pdf, to mobile-friendly .epub, or even our web version, with the content of the rulebook hosted on the site itself for you web enthusiasts! Grab some of the provided character sheets as well!

We also invite you to visit the forums, register and say hello! And for you world-builders and storytellers, try your hand at developing an Episode or campaign setting! Players and GM's, let us know how your MLP:RiM game(s) are doing! Give us feedback, or make art, or just get involved in general!

So enjoy My Little Pony: Roleplaying is Magic, Season Two Edition—and in the next several days, be on the lookout for the first of our giveaways of one of three 16" x 20" framed limited edition watercolor paintings done by Sketchbook for the S2E rulebook!

Thank you everyone for visiting our site and taking an interest in our project. We've worked hard to make S2E the best we can make it, and we hope you'll like it as much as we do. Happy roleplaying, my little ponies!

Your faithful game designers,

~The MLP:RiM Team
